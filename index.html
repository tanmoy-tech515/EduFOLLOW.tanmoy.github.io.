Frequently Asked Questions

How do i know right courses for me

Our courses are designed to cater to a wide range of interests and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, you'll find courses that suit your needs. You can explore our course catalog, read reviews, and watch previews to help you make an informed decision.

DO i get a refund if the course is not worth my money?

We offer a refund policy based on our terms and conditions. If you are not satisfied with a course, you may be eligible for a refund within 15-30 days of purchase. Please review our refund policy or contact support for assistance.

Will i have lifetime access to course i purchase?

Yes, when you purchase a course, you get lifetime access to it. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the content anytime. However, lifetime access applies as long as the course remains available on our platform.

Will you assest me to find a job ?

While we do not directly provide job placements, we offer career guidance, resume-building tips, and interview preparation resources to help you in your job search. Some courses may also include job assistance programs or internship opportunities. Check the course details for more information.

Do i get all source code from projects ?

Yes, if you purchase lifetime access to a course, you will receive the full source code for all included projects. You can download and use it for learning and personal projects. Please check the course details for any licensing restrictions.

what are your payment options ?

We accept multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, Razorpay, phonepay, and other secure online payment gateways. Depending on your location, additional payment options such as bank transfers or digital wallets may be available. Please check our checkout page for the full list of payment methods.

Do i get certificate from here?

Yes, we provide certificates for successfully completed courses. After finishing a course and meeting the necessary requirements, you can download your certificate from your account. If you have any issues, feel free to contact our support team.

what are the requirement to start from there ?

To start learning, you typically need a stable internet connection, a device (computer, tablet, or smartphone), and a willingness to learn! Some courses may have specific prerequisites, such as prior knowledge or software requirements, which will be mentioned in the course details
